Registration Information



  • HOW MUCH DOES WLC COST?

    For the entire weekend, including 5 meals, lodging, and other event gear (note: transportation not included), the cost is $138 per person, and there will be no price increases. Please note that your payment must be RECEIVED by October 31st.

    We are also offering an installment plan with the first installment of $85 due by October 31st and the second installment of $53 due by December 1st.
